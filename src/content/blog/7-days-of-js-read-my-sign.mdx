Welcome to day 3 of 7 of Days of JavaScript!

Today's challenge is to write a function that takes an array, and says how many numbers are positives, how many numbers are negatives, and how many are zeros.

Example:

```java
[1,3,0,-1,0,5,-5]
...
\{
zeros: 2,
positives: 3,
negatives: 2
}
```

Good luck!
